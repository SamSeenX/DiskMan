It's Free.
Forever.

I built DiskMan because I needed it. I know how annoying it is to run out of space and not know why. I want everyone to have valid tools to manage their digital life, without paywalls or subscriptions.

Is there a catch?

No. There is no catch. You can download it, use it, and never pay a dime.

However... developing this takes time, effort, and a suspicious amount of caffeine.

The people who donate are the ones keeping open source projects alive. They are the unsung heroes. The legends. You could just use it for free without supporting the developer... and that's fine. (I mean, do you really want to be that person? Just kidding. Mostly.)
